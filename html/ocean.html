About Coral Reafs

Coral bleaching can occur during periods of elevated temperatures in the ocean. Near-real time and forecasted coral bleaching alert levels are used to anticipate bleaching events. Chlorophyll data is also available here and can identify coral spawning events.

About Fisheries

The information provided here can inform fishery departments on the state of the upcoming tuna fishing season for the purpose of licensing and implementing sustainable fishing practices. Local fishermen can use the front detection maps to identify areas of possible upwellings that have high levels of biological activity.

About Sea Level

Maps for sea level changes across the Pacific are available from 1950 to present in a relatively low resolution, and a higher resolution from 1993 to present.

  • Reconstruction - low resolution (1950 to present)
  • Altimetry - high resolution (1993 to present)

The Real Time Data Display shows sea level information, air & water temperature, humidity, wind speed and wind direction. Data is shown in real-time, going back as far as four days.
